About Our Lodge

The Knights of Pythias is a fraternal organization founded in 1864 in Washington, D.C., by Justus H. Rathbone. The order was the first fraternal organization to receive a charter under an act of the United States Congress. It was founded the principles of Friendship, Charity, and Benevolence.

Our lodge continues this proud tradition, bringing together members who are dedicated to these principles and to serving our community. Through various charitable activities, social events, and educational programs, we strive to make a positive impact on the lives of our members and the broader community.

Membership in the Knights of Pythias is by invitation and is open to individuals who share our values and commitment to service. Members enjoy a sense of brotherhood, opportunities for personal growth, and the satisfaction of contributing to meaningful charitable causes.

Our regular meetings provide a forum for fellowship, planning community service activities, and continuing the traditions that have sustained our order for over 150 years.
